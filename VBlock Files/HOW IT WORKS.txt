Little Tutorial how to run VBlock correctly:

Simply ignore all the other files and run
VBLOCK.exe as administrator!

Or: open the shortcut of VBLOCK.exe

You can move the shortcut to the location
you prefer!

To fix it on taskbar just open the program
then right click on the icon on the task bar
and tap pin to task bar.
